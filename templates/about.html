About Accidetect: Automated Road Accident Detection System

Welcome to Accidetect, our automated road accident detection system designed to revolutionize the way insurance agents handle accident investigations. Accidetect utilizes YOLO (You Only Look Once), a state-of-the-art object detection algorithm, to swiftly and accurately identify incidents of road accidents within CCTV footage.

Our Mission

At Accidetect, our mission is clear: to streamline the process of gathering evidence for insurance claims in the aftermath of road accidents. By harnessing the power of artificial intelligence and computer vision, we aim to empower insurance agents and law enforcement officials with efficient tools to expedite their investigations, ultimately providing quicker resolution for claimants.

How It Works

Accidetect operates seamlessly, enabling users to simply upload CCTV footage to our web application. Upon analysis, our algorithm meticulously scans the video feed, pinpointing exact timestamps where road accidents occur. This eliminates the need for manual review of lengthy footage, saving valuable time and resources for insurance agents.

Key Features

  • Accurate Detection: Accidetect's YOLO-based detection system ensures precise identification of road accidents, minimizing false positives and false negatives.
  • Time Efficiency: By automating the process of identifying accident timestamps, Accidetect drastically reduces the time required for insurance agents to gather evidence.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Our intuitive web application makes it easy for users to upload footage and retrieve accident timestamps effortlessly.
